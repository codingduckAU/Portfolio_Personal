Who is Daniel?

Ever since I was young, I was creating websites.
Back in 1999 on GeoCities.com I lauched my first, it was all the craze if you knew how to code in
only HTML. Not many web building tools were available back then, and words like CSS &
JavaScript were rarely spoken.

Upon leaving school I studied 3D Animation to explore how creative I could be.
But being a youngster I wanted to explore my entreprenual side and went onto launching start-ups
and building businesses. While creating wordpress websites for my companies and others.

Now it is time to fulfil my lifetime passion of making websites from scratch, and mixing it with 2D &
3D animation to become a creative front-end developer.

And yes, I have a duck fascination... #codingDuckAU
